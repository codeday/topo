## Gradients

Gradients come in one shade. Rather than specifying the shade of a gradient, you specify its angle, e.g. `taffy.90`.
Allowed angles are 0, 30, 45, 60, 90, 120, 135, 150, 180, 210, 240, 270, 300, or 330.

  <Story name="Gradients">
